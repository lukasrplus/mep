Basic Beliefs

We believe that a variety of different mobile payment models will emerge and operate successfully in different geographic regions, since different existing payment ecosystems and dominant market positions of established players determine the options for innovative start-ups.

Innovation in the mobile payment sphere will be delivered primarily by start-ups. We believe that the companies dominating the mobile payment industry 10 years from now are still at an early stage today or have not even been founded. The main criteria for the success of these companies:

● Co-operation among peers and with established players (co-opetition)
● Integration of existing components
● Simplicity, security and speed, and, first and foremost
● A strong focus on customer experience and user interfaces

Approach

MEP Mobile Equity Partners GmbH is a business development hub specialising in seed and early-stage venture investments in the mobile payment sector. We pay particular attention to ensuring genuine synergies and complementary relationships between the cluster companies. Based in Vienna, MEP acquires stakes in innovative start-ups in the DACH and CEE region with the potential to become a European or worldwide player. In our selection process, we favour companies with clear business models and strong co-operation strategies.

What distinguishes us from other Fintech incubators is the fact that we take on active management functions as a sales and business development hub for the cluster companies and thereby assuming accountability for results. Managing collaborative innovation, enabling coopetition, multi-stakeholder facilitation and a strong focus on customer experience and user interfaces are the key concepts characterizing our approach.
